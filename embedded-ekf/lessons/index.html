  8. 0008 - Nominal Velocity And Position Prediction C target: rotate specific force, add gravity, integrate v and p.
  9. 0009 - Covariance Meaning C target: covariance storage, init, symmetry checks.
  10. 0010 - Linearized Error Dynamics C target: F and G matrix builders.
