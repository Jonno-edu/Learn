Lesson 0008: Nominal Velocity And Position Prediction

Once attitude is known, the estimator can rotate corrected accelerometer specific force into NED and integrate velocity and position.

The One Thing

Velocity and position prediction use the navigation-frame acceleration: 

f_b = accel_mps2 - b_a
a_n = rotate(q_nb, f_b) + g_n
v_n = v_n + a_n * dt_s
p_n = p_n + v_old * dt_s + 0.5 * a_n * dt_s * dt_s

Use v_old in the position equation so the integration method is explicit and testable.

NED Sign Sanity

Local NED has positive z down. The gravity vector is: 

g_n = [0, 0, 9.80665]

A stationary state should produce near-zero a_n. That test catches many sign and frame mistakes before GPS updates are introduced.

Quick Check

Why add gravity after rotating specific force?

The accelerometer correction is in the body frame. Gravity is a navigation-frame vector in this model, so the specific force is rotated first and gravity is added in NED.
